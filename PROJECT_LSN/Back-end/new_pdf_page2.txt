Article1—GeneralProvisions
This agreement is governed by Article 06 of Executive Decree No. 13-306 of 31 August 2013 on the organisation of practical
internships in a professional setting for students, and by the Order of 21 January 2015 on the nature and modalities of their
evaluation and scheduling.
Article2—PurposeoftheAgreement
This convention defines the organisational framework for the practical internship carried out by AdelChibi  at Cyber-Comp ,
formalising the rights, duties, and responsibilities of all parties.
Article3—ObjectivesoftheInternship
The internship enables the student to apply theoretical and methodological knowledge in a real professional environment, develop
professional competencies, and prepare for working life. The internship subject is: Hi.
Article4—InternshipThemeandWorkOrganisation
The theme, work plan, and assigned objectives are defined in consultation between the university and the host organisation, in line
with the student's study programme and the host institution's operational needs. The student shall produce a final internship
report upon completion.
Article5—Supervision
Each internship benefits from dual supervision:
An AcademicSupervisor  designated by University Constantine 1 Mentouri, responsible for pedagogical follow-up and
evaluation.
A HostSupervisor  designated by Cyber-Comp, responsible for daily on-site supervision.
The student is placed under the hierarchical authority of the host supervisor and must comply with the host institution's internal
regulations.
Article6—PracticalModalities
Duration: 1monthand1day  — from 01Jul2026  to 01Aug2026 .
The internship is carried out during academic year 2025/2026 .
Article7—StudentStatusandSocialProtection
The student retains their registered student status throughout the internship. Social security coverage is provided by University
Constantine 1 Mentouri. In the event of a work-related accident, the host institution must file the workplace accident declaration
with the relevant social security body and send a copy to University Constantine 1 Mentouri without delay.
Article8—Conduct,DisciplineandAbsence
The  student  is  subject  to  the  host  institution's  internal  regulations,  including  discipline,  working  hours,  safety  rules,  and
professional confidentiality. Any absence, even for medical reasons, must be justified within 72hours . In the event of serious
misconduct, the host institution may terminate the internship after informing the academic supervisor.
Article9—ConfidentialityandIntellectualProperty
Work results are the joint property of University Constantine 1 Mentouri and Cyber-Comp. The internship report must be
submitted to the academic supervisor after review by the host supervisor. The report may remain confidential but will be
accessible to teaching staff for evaluation. In case of publication, both institutions and supervisors must be credited.
Article10—RemunerationandBenefits
Unless agreed otherwise in writing, the student is not entitled to any remuneration from the host institution. The host may,
however, provide benefits in kind or a supplementary allowance in accordance with its policies and applicable regulations.•
